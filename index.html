Welcome!

I am Jieshuang Wang (王界双), an Alexander von Humboldt Fellow at Max-Planck-Institut für Kernphysik. My research interests are high-energy astrophysics with multi-messengers, including cosmic-rays, neutrinos, gravitational waves and their electromagnetic counterparts. I have experiences on analytical modelings, numerical simulations and data analysis.
